The Spencer-Churchill Royal Connection

The 1st Duke of Marlborough's daughter Anne married Charles Spencer, 3rd Earl of Sunderland — merging the Churchill and Spencer families. Their descendants became the Spencer-Churchill Dukes of Marlborough. On the Spencer side, the line continued separately through the Earls Spencer — eventually producing Diana, Princess of Wales, who married into the House of Windsor.

This means PM Winston Churchill (a Spencer-Churchill, descended from the Dukes of Marlborough) and Princess Diana (a Spencer, descended from the Earls Spencer) share the same 18th-century Spencer-Churchill ancestor — Charles Spencer, 3rd Earl of Sunderland.

And since Mike's line connects to the Bailiff of Dorchester — the common ancestor of both the Marlborough and Spencer-Churchill branches — Mike is a very distant cousin not only of Winston Churchill, but through the Spencer-Churchill link, of Princess Diana and the modern British Royal Family.
